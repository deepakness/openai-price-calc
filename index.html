As OpenAI bills you based on the number of tokens sent in your prompt plus the number of tokens returned by the API, I am taking an assumption of 100 words of prompts length for each 1000 words generated by the API. And I am adding that cost to the final cost as well.

If your prompt length is more than the assumed value of 100 words per 1000 generated words, you can enter the value in the above field. And the final estimated price will be updated.

How does it work?

This calculator takes the information provided on the official OpenAI website to calculate the estimated price based on the number of words. The calculator assumes that 1000 tokens are about 750 words, as OpenAI recommends.
