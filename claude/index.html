As Anthropic bills you based on the number of tokens sent in your prompt plus the number of tokens returned by the API, I am taking an assumption of prompt length of 100 words for each 1000 words generated by the Claude API. I am adding that cost to the final cost as well.

But if your prompt length is different than the assumed value of 100 words per 1000 generated words, you can enter the value in the above field. And the final estimated price gets updated.

For Claude v1 Instant and Claude v1 models, the prompt prices are $0.43/million characters and $2.90/million characters respectively.

How does this Anthropic Claude price calculator work?

This calculator takes the information provided on the official Anthropic website to calculate the estimated price based on the number of words.
